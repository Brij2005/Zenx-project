Study With AI Planner

Study With AI Planner is an intelligent study companion designed to help students plan, track, and optimize their learning. By leveraging AI, it creates personalized study schedules, sets reminders, and provides insights to improve productivity and retention.

Features

Personalized Study Plans – Generates schedules based on your subjects, topics, and available time.

AI-Powered Recommendations – Suggests study priorities and breaks for maximum focus.

Progress Tracking – Visual dashboards show completion rates, study streaks, and upcoming tasks.

Task Reminders – Automated notifications to keep you on track.

Adaptive Learning – Adjusts your plan based on performance and learning speed.

Integration – Syncs with calendars and other productivity tools.
